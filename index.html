FROM DESK OF PRINCIPAL

RAJENDRA UNIVERSITY BALANGIR

Rajendra (Autonomous) College, Balangir functioning from 1944 as the premier undergraduate college of Western Odisha has, over the years, grown to be a renowned institution of Higher Education with thirteen Post-Graduate Departments in Arts, Science and Commerce, including M.Phil. courses in seven of them. The college has been catering to the ever-increasing needs and aspirations of a huge population of the state spread across six to eight of its largely backward districts. The college aims to produce well-informed and enlightened individuals who, not inferior in point of education and accomplishments, would rise to situations of trust and respectability in life and contribute handsomely to the stupendous tasks of building a resurgent India. It endeavours to transform its alumni into ladies and gentlemen of real merit, worth and character who would have every disposition to act uprightly in giving leadership to the society. As the institution stands at the cusp of a historic juncture of its journey by marking seventy-five years of its glorious existence as a public institution, we are proud to host our renovated and dynamic website in keeping with the needs of the New Age. Hope visitors and users of the portal will find useful information about the College that may help them in optimum utilization of its facilities, resources and services. A cordial welcome to all !

(Prof.B K Jena)
Principal

ABOUT US

Rajendra University(Balangir)

Rajendra College (Autonomous), Balangir, a premier centre of education of Odisha, was one of the first new Colleges not only to be affiliated to Sambalpur University set up in 1944 but a significant Constituent College that the incipient University required for its own recognition as the first University of the State. The College has a hallowed history and a sprawling geography of it own. The College is situated at a distance of four kilometers from the Balangir Railway Station towards Patnagarh and Harishankar and is adjacent to the State highway no 43. At present, the College commands a huge campus with separate buildings for the faculties of Arts, Science and Commerce. Located at an idyllic setting, the College stands aloof from the din and bustle of the town and therefore makes itself an ideal destination for the quiet and serenity of higher education. Surrounded by ancient hills that lend it a green panoramic view, the College never fails to exalt the spirit of its students and staff towards pursuing excellence in their chosen areas. Notwithstanding some of the temporary setbacks and obscurations, Rajendra College (Autonomous) has a proud record as a pillar of eminence in the educational improvement of Western Odisha. As Balangir is officially recognised as a Backward District of the State of Odisha, it needs to tone up its resources in general and human resources in particular.
